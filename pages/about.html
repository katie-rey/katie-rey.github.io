About Me (well not really...meet Boston)

This is our puppy Boston. We rescued him from the pound after he was taken from our neighbour by the dog catchers. We wanted to keep him in the community so we brought him home.

He's a soft gentle boy with the energy and lust for life of two dogs. He loves long walks on the beach, roaming the wild west and playing fetch.

His favourite toy is a squeaky turtle which he loves to 'kill' and his favourite food is peanut butter.
