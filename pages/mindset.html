Neuroplasticity


Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people


Understanding neuroplasticity benefits people by allowing them to understand our brains are ‘plastic’ and malleable. By understanding this notion, you can change your mindset from “my brain isn’t wired to do this, I can’t do it” to “I can do this if I try, I can rewire the neuron pathways in my brain”.


Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your own benefit


I would engage through practice, practice and more practice of difficult concepts to help strengthen pathways in my brain.


What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?


You can increase your neuroplasticity through a number of ways:


  1. Practice, practice, practice
  2. Cognitive exercise (playing games!!!)
  3. Having clarity and focus around what I’m trying to achieve
  4. Keep working on adopting a growth mindset
  5. Practice mindfulness
  6. Increase exercise
